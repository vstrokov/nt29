This demo showcases a sophisticated scroll-triggered animation where an image scales and text content overlaps it smoothly.

Beautiful Landscape

Overlapping Content

This text content starts below the image but begins to overlap as you scroll. Once you've scrolled past 80% of the scaled image height, this content "attaches" to the image and they scroll together as one combined block.

The transition is smooth and visually engaging, creating a sophisticated scroll experience without any layout jumps or glitches.
